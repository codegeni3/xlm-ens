# xlm-ens Disaster Recovery Drill Runbook

This document outlines the procedures for conducting quarterly disaster recovery drills on the testnet.

## 1. Drill Preparation

### 1.1. Schedule the Drill

A date and time for the drill will be scheduled and communicated to all relevant team members.

### 1.2. Define the Scenario

A specific disaster scenario will be chosen for the drill. Examples include:

*   A critical bug is discovered in the registrar contract.
*   Admin keys are suspected to be compromised.
*   The frontend is experiencing a DDoS attack.

### 1.3. Prepare the Testnet Environment

The testnet environment will be prepared to simulate the chosen disaster scenario.

## 2. Drill Execution

### 2.1. Initiate the Drill

The drill will be initiated by the designated drill coordinator.

### 2.2. Follow the Disaster Recovery Plan

The team will follow the procedures outlined in the `disaster-recovery.md` document to respond to the simulated disaster.

### 2.3. Document All Actions

All actions taken during the drill will be documented in a shared document.

## 3. Drill Review

### 3.1. Post-Drill Debrief

A debrief meeting will be held after the drill to discuss what went well and what could be improved.

### 3.2. Update the Disaster Recovery Plan

The `disaster-recovery.md` document and any associated scripts will be updated based on the lessons learned from the drill.

### 3.3. Share the Results

A summary of the drill results will be shared with the wider team.
